= Troubleshooting {Insights} in {RHCloud}

[role="_abstract"]
You can troubleshoot issues with {Insights} in the {RHCloud} by checking logs and certificates.

To troubleshoot hosts monitoring, you can try the following approaches:

* You can find the logs in the `/var/log/foreman/production.log` file.
* If you have problems connecting to {Insights}, ensure that your certificates are up-to-date.
Refresh your subscription manifest to update your certificates.
