= Unassign Ansible roles from a host by using {ProjectWebUI}

[role="_abstract"]
You can unassign Ansible roles from your host by using {ProjectWebUI}.

.Prerequisites
* Your {Project} account has a role that grants the `create_ansible_lifecycle_environment_paths`, `edit_ansible_lifecycle_environment_paths`, `view_ansible_content`, `view_ansible_lifecycle_environment_paths`, `view_ansible_lifecycle_environments`, and `view_organizations` permissions.

.Procedure
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Hosts* > *All Hosts*.
. In the list of hosts, select your host.
. On the *Ansible Director* tab, click the *Content* tab.
. From the list of assigned Ansible roles, select an Ansible role and click on the *Left Arrow* icon.
. Click the *Save* icon to submit your changes.

.Verification
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Ansible* > *Environments*.
. Verify that {Project} shows your newly assign Ansible Execution Environment for your selected Ansible lifecycle environment.
