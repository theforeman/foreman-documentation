= Assign Ansible content to an Ansible lifecycle environment by using {ProjectWebUI}

[role="_abstract"]
You can assign Ansible content to an Ansible lifecycle environment by using {ProjectWebUI}.
Ansible content consists of imported Ansible collections and roles in specific versions.

.Prerequisites
* Your {Project} account has a role that grants the `create_ansible_lifecycle_environment_paths`, `edit_ansible_lifecycle_environment_paths`, `view_ansible_content`, `view_ansible_lifecycle_environment_paths`, `view_ansible_lifecycle_environments`, and `view_organizations` permissions.
* Your {Project} contains an Ansible lifecycle environment path.
* Your {Project} contains Ansible content.

.Procedure
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Ansible* > *Environments*.
. For your Ansible lifecycle environment, click the *Ansible* icon.
. From the list of Ansible content, select versions of Ansible content that you want to use for this lifecycle environment.
. Click *Confirm* to assign content to your lifecycle environment.

.Verification
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Ansible* > *Environments*.
. Verify that {Project} shows your newly assigned Ansible content for your Ansible lifecycle environment.
