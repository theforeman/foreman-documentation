= Enabling power management on hosts

[role="_abstract"]
Enable the baseboard management controller (BMC) module on {ProductName} to perform remote power management tasks on hosts, including power cycling and monitoring power states.

{ProjectName} supports the following BMC providers:

* Intelligent Platform Management Interface (IPMI)
* Redfish

{ProjectName} supports the following IPMI implementations:

* `freeipmi`
* `ipmitool` (default)

.Prerequisites
* Your host has a network interface of the BMC type.
{ProductName} uses this NIC to pass credentials to the host.

.Procedure
. Enable the BMC module:
+
[options="nowrap", subs="+quotes,attributes"]
----
ifndef::containerized[# {foreman-installer} --foreman-proxy-bmc "true"]
ifdef::containerized[# {foremanctl} deploy --add-feature bmc]
----
. Optional: Select the IPMI implementation:
+
[options="nowrap", subs="+quotes,attributes"]
----
ifndef::containerized[# {foreman-installer} --foreman-proxy-bmc-default-provider "_freeipmi_"]
ifdef::containerized[# {foremanctl} deploy --bmc-ipmi-implementation "_freeipmi_"]
----
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Infrastructure* > *Subnets*.
. Select the subnet of your host.
ifdef::satellite[]
. On the *{SmartProxies}* tab, select your {ProductName} as *BMC {SmartProxy}*.
endif::[]
ifndef::satellite[]
. On the *Proxies* tab, select your {ProductName} as *BMC Proxy*.
endif::[]
. Click *Submit*.

.Next steps
* Configure a BMC interface on your host.
For more information, see {ManagingHostsDocURL}configuring-a-baseboard-management-controller-interface[Configuring a baseboard management controller (BMC) interface] in _{ManagingHostsDocTitle}_.
