= Configuring {Project} for {Insights} analytics

[role="_abstract"]
ifdef::satellite[]
You can configure {Project} to connect to {Insights} hosted in the {RHCloud} or to local {insights-iop} so your hosts receive suggestions for improvement.
endif::[]
ifndef::satellite[]
You can configure {Project} to connect to {Insights} hosted in the {RHCloud} so your {RHEL} hosts receive suggestions for improvement.
endif::[]
