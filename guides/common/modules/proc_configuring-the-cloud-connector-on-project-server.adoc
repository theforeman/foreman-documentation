= Configuring the cloud connector on {ProjectServer}

[role="_abstract"]
You can configure the cloud connector on your {ProjectServer} to upload host inventory to {RHCloud}.
By performing this action, you run a remote job on your {ProjectServer} to configure the cloud connector.

.Prerequisites
* You have added the SSH key to authorized keys on your {ProjectServer}.
For more information, see the xref:adding-{project-context}-server-ssh-key-to-authorized-keys_{context}[].

.Procedure
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *{Insights}* > *Inventory Upload*.
. Click *Configure cloud connector*.
. Click *Confirm*.

.Verification
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Monitor* > *{Project} Tasks* > *Tasks*.
. Find the job with the name `Remote action: Configure cloud connector`.
. Verify that the job completed successfully.
