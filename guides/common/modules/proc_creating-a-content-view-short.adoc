= Creating a content view

[role="_abstract"]
Create a content view to define which repository versions are available to hosts in your organization.
Content views enable version control and selective content distribution across lifecycle environments.

.Procedure
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Content* > *Lifecycle* > *Content Views*.
. Click *Create content view*.
. In the *Name* field, enter `CentOS Content View`.
. In the *Description* field, enter a description for the content view.
. Select *Content view* or *Composite content view* as the *Type*.
. Click *Create content view*.
