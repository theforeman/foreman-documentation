= Delete an Ansible Execution Environment by using {ProjectWebUI}

[role="_abstract"]
You can delete an Ansible Execution Environment from {Project} by using {ProjectWebUI}.

.Prerequisites
* Your {Project} account has a role that grants the `destroy_ansible_execution_environments`, `view_ansible_content`, `view_ansible_execution_environments`, and `view_organizations` permissions.

.Procedure
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Ansible* > *Execution Environments*.
. For your Ansible Execution Environment, click the *Delete* icon.
. Click *Confirm* to delete your Ansible Execution Environment from {Project}.

.Verification
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Ansible* > *Execution Environments*.
. Verify that {Project} does not contain your deleted Ansible Execution Environment.
