= Promoting your content view to the new lifecycle environment

[role="_abstract"]
Promote content views to lifecycle environments to make specific content versions available to hosts in each environment.
Content view promotion enables controlled rollout of software updates across your infrastructure.

.Procedure
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Content* > *Lifecycle* > *Content Views*.
. Select the content view.
. On the *Versions* tab, click *Promote* for desired version under the Actions column.
. Select *Production* lifecycle environment from the available promotion paths.
. Add an optional *Description*.
. Click *Promote Version*.
