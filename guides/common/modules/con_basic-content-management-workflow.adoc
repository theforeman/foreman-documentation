= Basic content management workflow

[role="_abstract"]
Synchronize and manage Enterprise Linux content on your hosts to control what software is available across your infrastructure.
Content management with {Project} and Katello enables version control and lifecycle management for your systems.

* Create {customrepo} to mirror content from CentOS Stream.
* Synchronize the Content.
* Create `Activation Keys`.
* Register Hosts to consume this content.
