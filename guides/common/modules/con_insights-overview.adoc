= {Insights} overview

[role="_abstract"]
{Insights} is a service that collects data from your hosts and sends it to the {RHCloud} for analysis.

If you use {Insights}:

* The Insights client collects data of your hosts and {Project} forwards the data through the Insights client to the {RHCloud} to calculate recommendations.
* You can view and remediate those recommendations both in the {ProjectWebUI} and in the {RHCloud}.
* {Project} uploads host inventory reports to the {RHCloud}.
