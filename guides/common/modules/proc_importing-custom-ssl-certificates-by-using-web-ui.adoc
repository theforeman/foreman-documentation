= Importing custom SSL certificates by using {ProjectWebUI}

[role="_abstract"]
Import SSL certificates and keys into content credentials before synchronizing {customcontent} from upstream servers that use HTTPS or client authentication.
This might include client certificates and keys or CA certificates.

.Procedure
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Content* > *Content Credentials*.
Click *Create new*.
. In the *Name* field, enter a name for your SSL certificate.
. From the *Type* list, select *Certificate*.
. In the *Content* field, paste your SSL certificate.
Alternatively, click *Choose file* to upload the certificate file.
. Click *Create*.
