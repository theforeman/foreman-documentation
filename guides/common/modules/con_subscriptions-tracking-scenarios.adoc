= Subscriptions tracking scenarios

[role="_abstract"]
You can track your subscription usage in one of the following scenarios, depending on your environment and data sensitivity.

Connected scenario::
You share all collected data with Red{nbsp}Hat.

* {Project} uploads inventory data to the {RHCloud} automatically.
* {RHCloud} provides all services.
* Red{nbsp}Hat Lightspeed analyzes the data.
* Subscriptions service in the {RHCloud} calculates utilization data.

Connected data-sensitive scenario::
You share just-enough data with Red{nbsp}Hat to enable subscription reporting.

* You control what data is uploaded by enabling obfuscation or exclusion settings.
* {Project} uploads inventory data to the {RHCloud} automatically.
* {RHCloud} provides limited services.
* Subscriptions service in the {RHCloud} calculates utilization data.

Disconnected scenario::
You do not share any data with Red{nbsp}Hat.
Instead, you track your subscription usage as follows:

* You can get usage data from the *Host - Installed Products* report in {Project}.
* You can get capacity data from the Subscriptions service in the {RHCloud}.
* You can calculate utilization data locally using a tool of your choosing.
