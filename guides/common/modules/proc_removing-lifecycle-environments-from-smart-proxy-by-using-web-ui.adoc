= Removing lifecycle environments from {SmartProxyServer} by using {ProjectWebUI}

[role="_abstract"]
When lifecycle environments are no longer relevant to hosts or environments are added incorrectly to {SmartProxyServer}, you can remove the lifecycle environments from {SmartProxyServer}.

You can use the {ProjectWebUI} to remove lifecycle environments from {SmartProxyServers}.

.Procedure
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Infrastructure* > *{SmartProxies}*.
. Select the {SmartProxyServer} that you want to remove a lifecycle from.
. Click *Edit* and click the *Lifecycle Environments* tab.
. From the right menu, select the lifecycle environments that you want to remove from {SmartProxyServer}, and then click *Submit*.
. To synchronize content to your {SmartProxyServer}, click the *Overview* tab, and then click *Synchronize*.
. Select either *Optimized Sync* or *Complete Sync*.
