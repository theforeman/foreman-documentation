= Uploading Salt reports manually

[role="_abstract"]
As an alternative to enabling automated Salt report uploads, you can upload Salt reports from your Salt Master to {Project} manually.

.Procedure
* Upload Salt reports from your Salt Master to {Project}:
+
// Python version depends on Salt version
[options="nowrap" subs="attributes"]
----
# /opt/saltstack/salt/bin/python3.10 /usr/sbin/upload-salt-reports
----
