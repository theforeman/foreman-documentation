= Removing Red Hat subscriptions from subscription manifests

[role="_abstract"]
You can remove Red{nbsp}Hat subscriptions from manifests to adjust entitlement allocations or decommission Red{nbsp}Hat products from your organization.

[WARNING]
====
Manifests must not be deleted.
If you delete the manifest from the Red Hat Customer Portal or in the {ProjectWebUI}, all of the entitlements for all of your hosts will be removed.
====

.Prerequisites
* A Red{nbsp}Hat subscription manifest is imported to {ProjectServer}.
For more information, see xref:importing-red-hat-subscription-manifests-into-{project-context}[].

.Procedure
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, ensure the context is set to the organization you want to use.
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Content* > *Subscriptions*.
. On the row of each subscription you want to remove, select the corresponding checkbox.
. Click *Delete*, and then confirm deletion.
