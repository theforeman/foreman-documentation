= Delete versions of imported Ansible content by using {ProjectWebUI}

[role="_abstract"]
You can delete versions of imported Ansible content from {Project} by using {ProjectWebUI}.

.Prerequisites
* Your {Project} account has a role that grants the `destroy_ansible_content`, `view_ansible_content`, and `view_organizations` permissions.

.Procedure
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Ansible* > *Content*.
. Click the *arrow* symbol next to your imported Ansible content.
. For the version that you want to delete, from the options menu, click *Delete*.
. Click *Confirm*.

.Verification
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Ansible* > *Content*.
. Click the *arrow* symbol next to your imported Ansible content.
. Verify that the version you have deleted is not part of the list anymore.
