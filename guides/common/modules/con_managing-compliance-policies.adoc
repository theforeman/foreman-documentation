= [[compliance-policy-deployment-options_security-compliance]]Managing compliance policies

[role="_abstract"]
A compliance policy is a scheduled audit that checks the specified hosts for compliance against a specific XCCDF profile from a SCAP content.
Before you can deploy compliance policies to scan your hosts, you must define these policies on {Project}.
