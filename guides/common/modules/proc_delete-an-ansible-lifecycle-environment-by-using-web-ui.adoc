= Delete an Ansible lifecycle environment by using {ProjectWebUI}

[role="_abstract"]
You can delete an Ansible lifecycle environment by using {ProjectWebUI}.

.Procedure
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Ansible* > *Environments*.
. In your Ansible lifecycle environment path, click the *Edit* icon.
. In your Ansible lifecycle environment, click the *Delete* icon.
. Click *Confirm* to delete your Ansible lifecycle environment.

.Verification
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Ansible* > *Environments*.
. For your Ansible lifecycle environment path, verify that {Project} no longer shows your previously deleted Ansible lifecycle environment.
