= Subscriptions usage data

[role="_abstract"]
For the purposes of spend management, you might be interested in the following data.

Usage::
Measures the consumption of installed Red{nbsp}Hat products.
+
.How much am I using
====
I have 42 hypervisors, each with 2 sockets, tagged with the Production usage type.
====

Capacity::
Measures the amount of Red{nbsp}Hat products that have been purchased.
It is the upper limit of usage.
+
.How much have I purchased
====
I purchased QTY 52 of RH00001.
Each RH00001 covers a 2-socket hypervisor and all of its guests.
====

Utilization::
Measures how much of the purchased capacity has been exhausted.
+
.How much of my capacity is being used
====
I am using 42 of my 52 RH00001, leaving 10 free for additional use.
====
