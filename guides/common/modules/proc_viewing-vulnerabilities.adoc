= Viewing vulnerabilities

You can view Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures (CVE) from the {vulnerabilityengine}.
You can examine how the CVEs affect your hosts.

:FeatureName: {vulnerabilityengine}
include::snip_technology-preview.adoc[]

.Prerequisites
* You have installed the {insights-iop}.
* Your {Project} account has a role that grants one of the following permissions: `view_foreman_rh_cloud` or `view_insights_hits`.

.Procedure
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Insights* > *Vulnerability*.
. In the *CVE ID* column, click the ID of the vulnerability to see details.
