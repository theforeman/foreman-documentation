= Service-specific journald identifiers

[role="_abstract"]
Each containerized {Project} service has a unique `journald` identifier that you use with `journalctl` to view its logs.
Depending on the service, you filter by the `systemd` unit name (`-u`) or the syslog identifier tag (`-t`).

.{Project} service journald identifiers
[cols="2,2,1,2,3", options="header"]
|===
| Service
| journald identifier
| Filter type
| Logging mechanism
| Example command

| Foreman
| `foreman`
| `-u`
| stdout
| `journalctl -u foreman`

| Foreman Sidekiq
| `foreman-sidekiq@*`
| `-u`
| stdout
| `journalctl -u 'foreman-sidekiq@*'`

| {SmartProxy}
| `foreman-proxy`
| `-u`
| Direct JOURNAL
| `journalctl -u foreman-proxy`

ifdef::satellite,katello,orcharhino[]
| Candlepin
| `candlepin`
| `-u`
| stdout (ConsoleAppender)
| `journalctl -u candlepin`

| Valkey
| `valkey`
| `-u`
| stdout
| `journalctl -u valkey`
endif::[]

| httpd access
| `httpd-access`
| `-t`
| `systemd-cat`
| `journalctl -t httpd-access`

| httpd error
| `httpd-error`
| `-t`
| `systemd-cat`
| `journalctl -t httpd-error`

| httpd SSL access
| `httpd-ssl-access`
| `-t`
| `systemd-cat`
| `journalctl -t httpd-ssl-access`

| httpd SSL error
| `httpd-ssl-error`
| `-t`
| `systemd-cat`
| `journalctl -t httpd-ssl-error`

ifdef::satellite,katello,orcharhino[]
| Pulpcore SSL access
| `pulpcore-ssl-access`
| `-t`
| `systemd-cat`
| `journalctl -t pulpcore-ssl-access`

| Pulpcore SSL error
| `pulpcore-ssl-error`
| `-t`
| `systemd-cat`
| `journalctl -t pulpcore-ssl-error`

| Mirror access
| `mirror-access`
| `-t`
| `systemd-cat`
| `journalctl -t mirror-access`

| Mirror error
| `mirror-error`
| `-t`
| `systemd-cat`
| `journalctl -t mirror-error`
endif::[]
|===

[NOTE]
====
Services that use the `-u` filter type are Podman containers managed by `systemd`.
Services that use the `-t` filter type route logs through `systemd-cat` with custom syslog identifier tags.
====
