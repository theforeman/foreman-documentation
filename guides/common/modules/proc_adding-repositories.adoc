= Adding repositories

[role="_abstract"]
Add upstream repositories to your content view to include their packages in your managed content.
Repository selection enables you to control which content is available to hosts in your organization.

.Procedure
. In the {ProjectWebUI}, navigate to *Content* > *Lifecycle* > *Content Views*.
. Click *CentOS Content View*.
. Click the *Repositories* tab.
. Select repositories that you want to add.
. Click *Add Repositories*.
