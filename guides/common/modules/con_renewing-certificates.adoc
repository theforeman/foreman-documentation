[id="renewing-certificates"]
= Renewing certificates

[role="_abstract"]
ifdef::containerized[]
You can renew default self-signed server and client certificates as well as the default self-signed certificate authority on {ProjectServer} to prevent them from expiring.
You can also renew the custom SSL certificate as well as the custom certificate authority certificate on {ProjectServer} and on {SmartProxyServer}.
endif::[]
ifndef::containerized[]
You can renew the CA certificate on {ProjectServer} or the custom SSL certificate on {ProjectServer} as well as on {SmartProxyServer}.
endif::[]
