[id="renewing-certificates"]
= Renewing certificates

[role="_abstract"]
ifdef::containerized[]
You can renew self-signed server and client certificates on {ProjectServer} to prevent them from expiring.
You can also renew the custom SSL certificate on {ProjectServer} and on {SmartProxyServer}.
endif::[]
ifndef::containerized[]
You can renew the CA certificate on {ProjectServer} or the custom SSL certificate on {ProjectServer} as well as on {SmartProxyServer}.
endif::[]
